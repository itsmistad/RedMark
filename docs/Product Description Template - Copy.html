How To Clean

  1. For removing odors — Vacuum the pillow thoroughly, lay it on a flat surface, and generously coat the pillow in baking soda. Let it sit overnight before vacuuming it away.
  2. For removing stains — Blot the stain with water, apply boric acid or any alternative stain remover, and keep blotting until the stain has lifted.
  3. For cleaning regularly — Soak the pillow in warm water with a light amount of laundry detergent for 10 minutes, gently squeeze out most of the water, run warm water over the pillow to remove excess detergent, and leave it in a well-ventilated area, such as outdoors on a sunny day, to dry.
